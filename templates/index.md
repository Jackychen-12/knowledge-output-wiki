## Dual-layer architecture

```
Knowledge layer (long-term compound)        Output layer (short-term packaging)
├── grows by topic                          ├── grows by scenario/channel
├── records "what I learned"                ├── packages "for this audience"
├── depth > polish                          ├── polish > completeness
└── compounds for years                     └── disposable after event
```
